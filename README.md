# adventureworks-sql-sales-analysis
SQL analysis project exploring internet sales performance using the AdventureWorks Data Warehouse dataset.
Internet Sales Analysis (SQL Project) of AdventureWorks.

Project Overview
This project compares the sales performance on internet sales based on the AdventureWorks Data Warehouse dataset.  
This is aimed at finding important business insights of the trends of the revenues, top performing products, customers and geographic sales distribution.

Business Questions
How many internet sales are being made?
Which are the most selling products?
What was the highest year of sales performance?
What are the highest revenue customers?
What are the top countries in terms of sales?

Tools & Technologies
Microsoft SQL Server
DBeaver
AdventureWorksDW Dataset

Key Insights
The product category that commands the highest revenue is the Bikes.
The highest point of Internet sales was observed in 2013 which was over $16 million.
The high-performing products and customers do not constitute a large segment of revenue.
The sales exhibit a consistent positive growth trend.
There are some geographical areas that are leading in terms of sales on the internet.

Skills Demonstrated
SQL Joins (multi-table joins)
Aggregations (SUM, AVG, GROUP BY)
Business KPI analysis
Data warehouse querying
Trend analysis
